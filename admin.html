Evaluation Guide

AI Thinking (Logic & Optimization)

Review the daily history. Do their inputs show a clear strategy? Look at their observations: do they mention a hypothesis? (e.g., "pH is dropping, so I'm reducing nutrient input today"). This shows they understand cause and effect.

Out-of-the-Box Thinking & Creativity

How do their observations and actions change after a random negative event? Do they articulate a creative recovery plan? (e.g., "Pest attack hit hard. I'm cutting light costs to save budget and slightly increasing pH to improve nutrient uptake.")

Researching Ability

Do their observations mention external concepts? (e.g., "Read that lettuce prefers a pH closer to 5.8, so I'm aiming for that."). This demonstrates an ability to seek and apply new knowledge to the problem.
